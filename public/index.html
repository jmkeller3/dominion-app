How to Save Your List

  • In order to save or edit your list, you must be logged in.
  • Add Cards to your list by clicking or tapping them. You can also drag and drop cards into slots.
  • You can remove cards by clicking on them in your list or by dragging its replacement over it.
  • Use the filters to help select the best cards.
  • The rank filter is based Qvist's 2017 rankings. He is a world class dominion player who competes at the top level.
